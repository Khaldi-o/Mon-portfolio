# Context & role
Internal feedback synthesis. Role: Data Analyst / BI.

## Business objective
Turn verbatims into clear trends to guide HR actions.

## Data & sources
- Internal surveys
- Anonymized feedback

## Architecture
- Text cleaning pipeline
- Aggregation tables by theme

## Pipeline / processing
1. Verbatim normalization
2. Theme categorization
3. Aggregation by team and period

## Modeling / KPI
- Dominant themes
- Weak-signal evolution
- Participation rate

## Dashboard / results
- Theme map
- Team comparison
- Trend alerts

## Code snippets
```sql
SELECT theme, COUNT(*) AS feedback_count
FROM feedbacks
GROUP BY theme;
```
